# 💼 Salary Bonus Calculator

A beginner-friendly JavaScript project that calculates an employee's bonus based on their **years of service** and **performance rating**. The program also displays the employee's total salary after adding the bonus.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 💰 Calculate employee bonus
- 📅 Check years of service
- ⭐ Evaluate employee performance
- 📊 Display salary, bonus, and total salary
- ✅ Uses conditional statements for decision making

## 📋 Bonus Rules

| Years of Service | Performance | Bonus |
|-----------------:|-------------|-------|
| 5 years or more | Excellent | 20% |
| 5 years or more | Any other | 10% |
| Less than 5 years | Excellent | 5% |
| Otherwise | No Bonus |
